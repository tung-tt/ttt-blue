World

The world of starts with beings known as Gnorps, who were introduced through a cosmic entity known as, Gunther into our Galaxy. They were spread far and wide and some landed on the planet Earth. Over time land was formed with humans and gnorps developing with it, civilization was formed. Now different cities and life was spread around the planet in different countries and bases.

    This world is separated by 3 different countries all being, the First Country, the Second Country, and the Third Country. Our setting will take place in the Third Country as it's well know for it's lifestyle is the most peaceful among humans and gnorps.

  • Topia

    A city in the Third Country, flowing with bountiful rivers and filled with life at every point. Buildings with nature interwoven so seamlessly, this is the main hub of the Third Country. When anything of necessity is required, you best expect to find it in Topia.
